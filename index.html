GT Sport

This page is dedicated to my favorite video game

Grand Turismo Sport

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Great entry level and up to professional level racing sim
  • Online play
  • Car selection
  • Community
  • Olympic Event
  • Player progression and ranking
  • Graphics

Cons

  • No damage
  • AI racers could be better
  • Online penalty system changes with every update
  • Some online players take advantage of no damage
  • Some car brands not featured
